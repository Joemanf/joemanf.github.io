About me

I am a man of many passions, including education, travel, and smoked salmon. However, my greatest passion is coding. That is why I uprooted my entire career as a teacher to focus my future into one pursuit - software engineering. The languages I know include JavaScript, Node, Express, CSS, Python, PostgreSQL, Flask, and a slight dabbling into C#. I also work with HTML, pug, React, and Redux. Software Development is one of the best ways for me to express my creative instincts, and has led me to feel more fulfilled in life.

Passionate about coding

Ever since I was 3, I have had a deep love for computers and what they can accomplish. To be a part of a world where we are surrounded by technology is facinating to say the least. I am proud of the fact that I can talk to these computers and work with them to create beautiful things. I specialize in React and CSS, making fun, streamlined web applications for others to enjoy. Nothing makes me happier than to work in a field where my creativity can shine.
